# HR-Attrition-Dashboard

## Problem Statement 
The organization is ecperiencing a 22.9% attrition rate, with departures concentrated in specific tenure bands and departments. This project analyzes 457 employee records to identify where and why attrition is concentrated, and to recommend a targeted retention strategy — rather than relying on the dashboard's existing engagement/performance-based risk flag, which does not reliably predict who actually leaves.

## Dataset 
457 employee records (coursework/simulation dataset) with fields including Department, Role, Join/Exit Date, Salary, Performance Rating, Engagement Score, Training Hours, Tenure, and a derived Status (Active/Exited).
